A crowd of hooded figures stood before the ritual site. The leader, dressed in a jade green gown standing before their pupils, raised their arms, and their pupils followed in unison. Together, they chanted, and their divine voices echoed off the canyon walls. Their inspiring words inspired the trees in the forest to grow and grow, until their roots had soaked up all the available groundwater in a three kilometre radius. The locals in the nearby city of Ranshank, now deprived of running water, angrily smacked their desiccated lips and filed a formal legal complaint against the cultists. To avoid further escalation, the leader hired a lawyer, but because of a negative externality on legal advice in the area, which lead to a per-unit tax being applied by the local government to close the deadweight loss between the private marginal cost and the social marginal cost, the leader, who had recently run low on funds, had to resort to a mere wizard. However, wizards used quite dated albeit mystical technology, so when the wizard raised their arms, they instead summoned something much more thunderous: The beginning of gunn.app.
